Founder

Ms. Sneha

With over a decade of experience in woodworking and textile arts, Sneha has cultivated her passion for craftsmanship into a thriving business. Inspired by the simplicity of nature and the warmth of home, she infuses each piece with love and dedication.Sneha's journey into artisanal creation began in her grandfather's woodworking workshop, where she learned the art of shaping wood with patience and precision. Today, she leads Handi Home Haven with a commitment to preserving traditional techniques while embracing contemporary design sensibilities.Driven by a desire to bring joy and authenticity into people's homes, Sneha oversees the creation of every item with meticulous attention to detail. Beyond her role at Handi Home Haven, Sneha is dedicated to supporting local artisans and promoting sustainable practices in the industry. She believes in the power of community and collaboration to create a more vibrant and conscious world.Welcome to Handi Home Haven, where every piece is crafted with love and intention.
